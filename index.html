Investing Basics

Investing helps grow your money over time.

Investing means putting your money into assets like stocks or funds to generate returns and beat inflation over the long term.

📈

Stocks

Ownership in a company.

Stocks allow you to own a portion of a company. They offer high returns but come with higher risk and volatility.

🏦

Fixed Deposits

Safe and stable returns.

Fixed Deposits are low-risk investments offered by banks with guaranteed returns, suitable for conservative investors.

📊 Investment Insights

Short-term: Less than 2 years, low risk

Long-term: 5+ years, higher returns

Risk Levels: Low → FD | Medium → Mutual Funds | High → Stocks
